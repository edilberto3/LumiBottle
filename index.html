Our Story

Born from a simple idea

"We noticed people — including ourselves — simply forgot to hydrate."

LumiBottle was founded in 2026 by a group of health-conscious students from the Asian College of Technology – IEF in Cebu City. Instead of annoying phone alarms, we built a bottle that communicates through light.

Today we're the Philippines' leading smart hydration brand, trusted by students, professionals, athletes, and families nationwide.
